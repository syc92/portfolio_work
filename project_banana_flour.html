Banana Flour

Period: 2016

Expertise: package design

Client: Sun Seed Vitalics

Package Design for Sun Seed Vitalics Banana Flour

Sun Seed Vitalics is a biotechnology company. They produce organic crops and health food. In 2016, I was invited to design the package for one of their products – Sun Seed Vitalics Banana Flour. They wanted to promote this product to the younger generation, and attended to break the stereotype of healthy food is only for the elderly. I used abstract graphics to express the concept of banana flour, keeping the visual appearance neat and modern.
