Pick Card Pick Card

Pick Card Pick Card is a flip card game. Players have 5 seconds to remember the position of cards with the same color. After 5 seconds all the cards will be turned over, and then players have to pick the right card pair (with same color). Once players get the correct card pair, a Pikachu will show up on the card, and that is why it’s called Pick Card Pick Card (pika pika) – the sound of Pikachu!
