NTU RPC

NTU RPC stands for National Taiwan University Record Production Club, which is a student organization that aims to to gather talented musicians in NTU and provides them with in-depth music industry knowledge and training and connect them to other professionals to collaborate on music production projects. As visual presentation is also an important component of record production, they want to build a strong visual identity, and I was fortunate to have the chance to create the visual identity system for them.

Year: 2015
Client: NTU Record Production Club
Duration: 2 months
Role: Designer / Art Director

Ideation

The entire project started from the logo. We spent a lot of time discussing which direction we should go for. We had some in-depth discussion on what’s the vision of the organization, what are the stories they want to tell, and what do they want to achieve. My job at this stage is to help them clarify what they really want. The following image shows some of our drafts during discussion.

Concept

Eventually, there are four core values/goals of this organization: artists collaboration, record production, personal growth, and industrial-oriented education. Each of them was transformed into a basic pattern. The shape of the logo is derived from the shape of CD which is a symbol of music production. Each core value/goal takes a quarter of the logo and combines as a whole circle with similar style. Other than logo itself, I also made the customized Chinese typography for the logo as well.

Colors

Yellow, is a metaphor of “Golden Record”, which is an iconic color that means the greatest honor and highest quality in the music production industry. For the secondary color, I used dark blue to make a strong contrast with yellow, and also dark blue gives people impression of professional.
