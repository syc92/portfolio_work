Collection: Logo & Logotype

During my graphic designer career, making logo was a big part of my practice, and it was also what I was really interested in. Here are some of my selected logos.

Year: 2012 - 2015
Client: Multiple clients

Tokyo Dreaming

This is logo is made for my photo album, Tokyo Dreaming. I made the shape of the Chinese characters of Tokyo hollowed and puffed to express the feeling of a dream.

NTU Music Festival

The logo is made for the National Taiwan University (NTU) Music Festival. I used sharp and hard shape to emphasize the rythm.

Daan Park

This logo is a part of my Daan Park Branding and Wayfinding System Redsign project. The logo takes the 2D shape of the park (trapezoid) and combine it with the shape of the Chinese character that means forests, which is the feature of the Daan Park.

Save Hospital

Few years ago, the National Taiwan University Hospital was facing a shortage of medical resouce but the management level didn't not take action to solve the problem, and thus they ran a campaign to draw the society's attention to force the management level to make some change. This logo is made for that campaign.

Impression of Mongolia

I made a graphic design series for my impression of mongolia few years ago after I came back from my trip there. An interesting proverb I heard their is "although the montain looks so close to you, you can't get there even riding a horse." That is because that grassland of Mongolia is so wide. But the actually underlining meaning of this is, sometimes some thing looks easy, but it's hard to be done. I turned this proverb into a logotype to for my entire graphic design series.

Sun Seed

This is the logo I made for the Sun Seed brand, which produce healthy ingredient.
