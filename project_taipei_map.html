Taipei Map

A Tourist Map Made for Foreign Travelers

Client: Like It Formosa

Expertise: infographic, illustration

Period: 2015

Project Overview

Invited by Like It Formosa (LIF), together we created and published this map in 2015, aiming to help foreign travelers quickly learn Taiwanese culture and provide a tour guide of what to do and where to visit in Taipei. The content was organized by the LIF team, and I was responsible for the design, including layout and dimensions, visual style, and print.

Concept & Process

We wanted to create a map that is playful and not only contains information but also shows what Taipei is like. Therefore, we decided to embed the information in the cartoon style illustration of Taipei. I first put all the information in the map, roughly gauged dimensions of the map, and iterated on how to combine information and illustration and how to group them into different sections. After figuring out the layout, I sketched the Taipei street scenery, and finished the illustration on the computer.

Dimensions

To make sure that the map is easy to be carried by the travelers, I made the folded size of the map small enough that it can be fitted into a pocket.
