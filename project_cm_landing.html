CodementorX – Landing Page Revamp

CodementorX is one of the products of Codmentor. It is a freelance platform that helps clients hire talented software developers around the world. The previous landing page design of CodementorX was outdated and was not able to deliver our brand message to our client precisely. The goal of this project is to revamp the visual identity, refine the content, and improve user experience.

Year: 2018
Project type: Work project
Duration: 1 month
Role: Product designer

Final design

At the design stage, I spent most of my time testing out different layouts, and tried out different styles of illustration. Other than the landing page itself, I also created some sub pages that are related to it, including SEO pages, FAQ pages, and testimonial pages.

Conclusion

What can be done better

I wish I could have a better understanding of our clients. Designers in CodementorX don’t usually conduct thorough user research because of limited resources and time zone issue (the product team is not in the same time zone as most of the clients). Our understanding of users (both clients and developers) is accumulated from the interaction we have with them over the entire time. It works well in most of the time, but I believe that there might be some bias and we could discover more valuable insight by conducting interviews with clients.
